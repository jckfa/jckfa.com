Jack did an interview with Nick Benson, owner and creative director of The John Stevens Shop, a design and stone-carving business founded in 1705 by John Stevens in Newport, Rhode Island. Nick is the third generation of a family of calligraphers and stone-carvers, and has continued developing his craft for four decades. The discussion explores Nick’s process, his guerrilla art ambitions, and the dark side of social media.
